WordCamp Vienna 2019

  • April 27th and 28th, 2019
  • UNI Campus in the Altes AKH
  • Expecting 450 WordPress enthusiasts
  • Informal, community-organized event
  • 2 or 3 tracks of WordPress focused learning
  • More than 20 great speakers

There are many ways for you to get involved!

WordCamp Vienna is returning on April 27th and 28th, 2019! It will be at the same great venue,

UNI Campus in the Altes AKH (Spitalgasse 2, 1090 Vienna) and we expect 450 WordPress enthusiastic participants.

WordCamps are informal, community-organized events that should be attended by everyone who uses WordPress, and everyone who could/should use WordPress. This includes bloggers, designers, developers, authors, marketers, SEOs, businesses, startups, non-profits, consultants, entrepreneurs, etc.

There will be 2 or 3 tracks of WordPress focused learning, with more than 20 great speakers including a lot of sessions in german and english language. The additional Contributor day gives you a special opportunity to work together and learn from some of the best specialists of the WordPress community.

There are many ways for you to get involved!
